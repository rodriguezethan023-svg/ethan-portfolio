Late Night
Legends

Late Night Legends is a conceptual marketing campaign developed for Gopuff to increase late-night ordering behavior among college students, gamers, night shift workers, and young professionals.

The Challenge

When traditional retailers close their doors, late-night demographics like gamers and shift workers are just beginning their peak hours. The challenge was to position Gopuff as the essential 'always-awake' partner for these overlooked timeframes.

Creative Strategy

The campaign centers on the moment of friction when hunger strikes unexpectedly at 2 AM. By utilizing high-contrast, 'neon-noir' visual storytelling, we positioned Gopuff as the Hero of Convenience.
