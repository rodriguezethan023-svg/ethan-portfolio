The Background

FROM EL PASO TO SAN ANTONIO.
CREATING WITH PURPOSE.

I'm Ethan, a graphic designer and visual storyteller passionate about creating work that connects with people. Born and raised in El Paso, Texas, I eventually made my way to San Antonio, where I now live, create, and continue growing as a designer.

My journey into design wasn't a straight line. Before pursuing a creative career, I attended college in Colorado on a basketball scholarship. It was there that I met my wife, Brittany, who is originally from San Antonio. That chapter of my life taught me discipline, teamwork, and the value of pushing beyond my comfort zone—lessons that continue to influence my work today.

After returning to Texas and making San Antonio home, I studied graphic design at San Antonio College and began building my freelance business. Through logo design, brand identity systems, social media content, photography, and digital marketing projects, I've developed a passion for transforming ideas into visual experiences that feel intentional, memorable, and impactful.

Today, I create visual solutions that help ideas come to life. Whether I'm designing a brand identity, capturing compelling photography, developing data-driven dashboards, or creating content that tells a story, my goal is always the same: to create work that is meaningful, engaging, and built with purpose.
